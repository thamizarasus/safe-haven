Frequently Asked Questions

Safe-Haven connects donors with verified aid organizations and safehouses that need essential supplies.
You can donate food, clothing, toys, first-aid supplies, or contribute through a secure credit/debit payment.
Our platform coordinates directly with local safehouses and partner services to ensure timely delivery.
Many donations are eligible for tax deductions depending on the recipient organization. Please consult your tax advisor.
