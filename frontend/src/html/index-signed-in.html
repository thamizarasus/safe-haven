About Safe Haven

Safe Haven is a community-led platform built to connect people with compassion to those in need. Whether it's food, clothing, or first aid, our mission is to ensure that donations reach local safehouses and support networks in real time.

Founded during a university hackathon, our team came together to create more than an app—we built a bridge between generosity and survival. We're committed to maintaining transparency, empathy, and continuous innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Safe-Haven connects donors with verified aid organizations and safehouses that need essential supplies.
You can donate food, clothing, toys, first-aid supplies, or contribute through a secure credit/debit payment.
Our platform coordinates directly with local safehouses and partner services to ensure timely delivery.
Many donations are eligible for tax deductions depending on the recipient organization. Please consult your tax advisor.
Tracking will be available soon. Currently, you will receive a confirmation and recipient info after submission.
